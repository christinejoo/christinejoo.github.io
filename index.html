Reading Notes — 
A Handmade Web, J.R. Carpenter

Handmade web refers to web pages coded by hand, they challenge conventions through a personal aesthetic. The handmade web refers to manual labor of the physical body — through work by the hand (coding), brower becomes real. "Ironically, handheld devices have distanced us from the handmade web."

This reading makes me think about the style of a lot of Parsons faculty and students, pushing the expressivity of code more than the commerical aesthetic. Also, thinking about exploring different possibilites of the web especially as a student.
