Dodgers Brand Book

Project for Core 1:Typography, Parsons School of Design.

This Los Angeles Dodgers brand book covers the Dodgers history from the start as the Brooklyn Dodgers to their 2020 World Series Championship title as the Los Angeles Dodgers. This coffee table book captures the spirit of the Dodgers franchise with a Dodger blue and white color palette and bold images.
