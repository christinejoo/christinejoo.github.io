EmpowHER

Project for Core 2: Typography, Parsons School of Design.

EmpowHER is a fictionally-branded event created to empower female athletes. The 3-day convention, based in Los Angeles, consists of a day of workshops under the theme of "Confidence," a day of master classes under the theme of "Craft," and a final day of local events under the theme of "Community."

Day 1 of Confidence consists of a series of workshops, led by female athletes and a panel of women working in the sports industry, in hopes of inspiring aspiring female individuals through conversations around gender inequality in the sports industry as well as other uncommon topics. Day 2 of Craft features a series of master classes taught by professional athletes. Day 3 of Community is a day of smaller local events around Los Angeles that hope to inspire and spark a passion admist more females, free of cost.
